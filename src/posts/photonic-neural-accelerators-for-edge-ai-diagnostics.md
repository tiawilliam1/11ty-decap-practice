\## Overview

A prototype system was evaluated across three hospital-like deployment scenarios.



\## Key Results

\- 38% lower latency

\- 24% lower energy per inference

\- Stable performance under noisy input conditions



\## Relevance

The approach is suitable for patent strategy in optical computing, edge AI hardware, and medical-device software workflows.
